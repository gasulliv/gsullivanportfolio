About


Persona 5, with it's strong color palattes and in your face, ransom note font types, was without a doubt my favorite video games aesthetic of 2019 so of course I had to make something in tribute. The page features the same three tone red, black and whites used prominently in the game with ransom note type evocative of the 'calling cards' featured so prominently in the game. While it's just a design for now, live page implementation is on the way!

Elements from the game's site were used in this fan-page, you can go here to see that page.

FAST FACTS


This template was designed mostly in photoshop with plans to create it live in motion. It's current live site skeleton runs on Boostrap 4, SASS (ft. Compass Compiler) and Javascript to give the letters their ransom note type look.
