About


MeUndies has long since had one of my favorite branding looks. I loved the big, solid colors and crisp text that are always fun and super accessible. In homage to my favorite panty pals, I put together a mock landing page for their membership campaign. The landing page has just gotten it's update and it's tighter than your favorite pair of panties. Bright, solid colored backgrounds, colorful graphics and it's fully resposive

FAST FACTS


This updated version of the panties page was made with Bootstrap 4, SASS (ft. Compass compiler), Photoshop and Illustrator together to mainpulate the graphics. In it's first iteration the page had a video/gif for the header, but it's proved to be difficult to maintain so it was dropped to favor of creating a more cohesive color palatte and promoting one of their newer prints. It was also updated with a better set of media queries to better fit all screen types.
