About


I've always loved dogs and their growing popularity has led to an increase in dog sites of all kinds. Unfortunately, despite the growing demand, there doesn't seem to be a wide variety of sleek or interesting pet targeted site templates around so I decided to try my hand at making one myself. Mabari is a simple, three page site focused on dog harness sales. I attempted to use forest greens, browns and tans to bring out the nature loving aspect of hiking as well as incorporating a social aspect of it by allowing for an area for the owner to pull in instagram photos of customers wearing thier gear.
